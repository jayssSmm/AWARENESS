Who we are

A grassroots foundation built on collective action

InAmigos Foundation was founded on 23 September 2020 by Govind Shukla. It's registered as a Section 8 nonprofit under the Central Government, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, and active across states throughout India.

The foundation runs on a simple belief: real change happens when professionals, volunteers, and corporate partners work the same problem from different angles — a meal delivered, a child taught, an animal treated, a woman trained, a tree planted, a skill passed on.

  • Licensed Section 8 nonprofit, transparent and accountable to donors
  • 80G and 12A certified — donations qualify for tax deduction
  • CSR-1 registered, open to corporate partnership programs
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified for operational quality

Where the work happens

Our six flagship projects

Food & clothing relief
